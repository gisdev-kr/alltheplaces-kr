# Advertising policy

The project portal reserves a small non-invasive placeholder for possible future advertising under the approved `gisdev-kr.github.io` site. No publisher or slot ID is committed before approval.

Ad loading is optional and must fail open. Ad blocking must never prevent the map, POI data, filters, details, or downloads from working. The interface does not ask visitors to click advertisements and does not force anyone to disable an ad blocker.
